Resilience in public, growth in private

I don't quit. I adapt, I rebuild, and I keep going.

I am a first-generation college graduate and certified MWBE business owner known for resilience, discipline, and steady follow-through.

Contact Me Now Read My Story

I have never had the luxury of giving up. I worked my way through college on third shift, becoming the first in my family to earn a degree. I earned a full scholarship to law school — and when life asked more of me than I had to give, I made the hard call to step back. That wasn't the end of the story. It was the middle of it.

When the pandemic closed the door on my administrative career, I opened another one. I learned to build a business from nothing — through Operation Hope, through trial and error, through sheer will — until what started as a way to keep my pets fed became DivaCats LLC, a certified MWBE business built by all of us, together.

I'm still on a healing journey. I still show up anyway. That's not a slogan for me — it's a track record.

About Me

I'm proof that setbacks are not endings. I put myself through college working third shift in word processing — no family precedent, no shortcuts, just persistence. That same persistence carried me to law school on a full scholarship, and it carried me through the decision to withdraw during my third year when personal circumstances demanded it.

When the pandemic took away my administrative work, I didn't wait for permission to start over. I trained through Operation Hope, learned the fundamentals of running a business, and launched DivaCats LLC from home — selling pet treats on eBay so my pets could pay their share of rent, too. It was practical. It was also the beginning of something real: during that same period, I became New York City and New York State MWBE certified, turning a survival plan into a legitimate, recognized business.

I bring resilience that's been tested, resourcefulness that's been proven, and the follow-through to make something official out of something that started as necessity. I'm still healing. I'm still building. Both, at the same time — that's the point.

First-Gen College Graduate NYC & NYS MWBE Certified Founder, DivaCats LLC
